Buying Electricity Tokens

Calculate the amount of tokens you will get if you are a pre-paid customer on Homelight 20A or Homelight 60A.

To find out your Amps (A) and tariff, check your last token for a TI number. The TI number is 07 for Homelight 20A and 02 for Homelight 60A.

For every unit of electricity, Homelight 20A costs 248.53c/kWh and Homelight 60A costs 315.93c/kWh (including VAT).
